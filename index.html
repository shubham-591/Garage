Welcome to the Garage

A garage is a dedicated space, often attached to or separate from a house, designed for parking vehicles and storing tools or other equipment. It provides shelter for cars, protecting them from the elements and potential theft. Many garages also serve as workshops or storage areas, offering a versatile space for DIY projects and household maintenance.
